About College

The DVR & Dr. HS MIC College of Technology (established in 2002) is a NAAC 'A' grade accredited engineering institution located in Kanchikacherla, Andhra Pradesh. It offers undergraduate (B.Tech), postgraduate (M.Tech, MBA, MCA), and Diploma programs. The flagship B.Tech program offers modern specializations like AI & ML, AI & DS, and CSE.


Quick Facts at a Glance

  • Affiliation: Permanently affiliated to JNTUK (Kakinada) and approved by AICTE.
  • Location: NH 9, Vijayawada - Hyderabad Highway, Kanchikacherla.
  • Flagship Courses: Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Electronics & Communication.
  • Fees: B.Tech tuition is typically around ₹40,000 per year, and PG courses are ₹35,000 - ₹50,000 per year.
  • Facilities: The campus includes state-of-the-art labs, a 600-capacity auditorium, on-campus hostels, Wi-Fi, and an outdoor gym.

    • Placements
