Dr. T. Vamsee Kiran

“Don’t judge each day by the harvest you reap but by the seeds that you plant” states Robert Louis Stevenson.

As the quote succinctly suggests ‘our daily actions define our lives, career and future and they chart the path of our progress. The small acts that we do day in and day out adds up to make a big impact on our growth.’

Dr. T. Vamsee Kiran, Principal, enjoys two decades of vast experience and rich exposure as an academician and administrator. He has completed his schooling in Nirmala English Medium School, Mangalagiri. He did his intermediate in APRJC & B.Tech from Bapatla Engineering College. Having qualified in GATE exam, he pursued his M.E from PSG college of Technology, Coimbatore. Even as a student pursuing his M.E, he has the rare credential of completing 5 lakh DST sponsored project. He was awarded his doctoral degree by JNTU Hyderabad in the specialization of Power Electronics and Drives.

He began his career as Lecturer in Sir C R Reddy College of Engineering, Eluru during 2002 to 2004. Later he worked in KLCE(now KLU) from 2004 to 2005. His tryst with MIC College of Technology began in 2005 as Assistant Professor. By sheer dint of hard work, diligence and discipline, he climbed the ladder of administration as he was delegated with several responsibilities and he handled them with great élan and expertise. He was the Head of the department from 2008 to 2019. He also worked as Vice Principal from 2018 to 2019. He was ratified as Professor by JNTU Kakinada. In 2019, he joined GIET Engineering College, Rajahmundry as its Principal. In 2022, he took charge as Director-Academics at MIC College of Technology. And now from April 2023, he has taken over as Principal of the college.

Dr. T. Vamsee Kiran has 14 publications in various international journals & has presented 9 papers in national & international conferences. He attended 20 national level workshops in various Institutions of great repute. He is a member of Professional societies like IE & ISTE. His areas of interest include Multilevel Inverters and Electric Drives. At present, he is guiding two Ph.D., scholars registered at JNTU Kakinada. Dr.Vamsee was given ‘Best Teacher’ award by the management of MIC College of Technology in 2008. He was also recognized as ‘Esteemed Employee’ in the academic year 2016-17, in recognition of his continued commitment and valuable contribution to the organization.

Dr. Vamsee has delivered Guest Lectures, chaired sessions at various conferences, acted as judge and guest at several venues of technical events in various engineering colleges. He organized several events besides the regular academic work. Dr. Vamsee is well-versed with the NBA, NAAC and Autonomous related accreditation activities which are very essential for any engineering college.
